Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

Introduction

At DevTools, we prioritize the protection of your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and safeguarding of your information when you visit our website. We encourage you to carefully review this policy. By accessing or using our services, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect the following information that you voluntarily provide when using our services:

  • Contact information (email, name) when reaching out through our contact forms
  • Email address for newsletter subscriptions
  • Business information for enterprise service inquiries
  • Feedback and correspondence through support channels

Automatically Collected Information

Our systems automatically collect the following technical data:

  • Device specifications (browser type, version, operating system)
  • IP address and general location data
  • Usage analytics (page views, session duration, navigation patterns)
  • Traffic source information

How We Use Your Information

We process your information for the following purposes:

  • Service delivery and maintenance
  • User experience optimization and website performance analysis
  • Technical support and inquiry response
  • Opt-in marketing communications and service updates
  • Legal compliance and regulatory requirements

Security Measures

We employ industry-standard security protocols and encryption technologies to protect your information. Our security measures include regular security assessments, secure data transmission, and access controls. While we strive for maximum security, please understand that no digital transmission or storage system is entirely secure.

Data Processing and Tool Security

Our developer tools operate exclusively client-side within your browser. We maintain a strict no-storage policy for sensitive data: no keys, passwords, or cryptographic materials are ever transmitted to or stored on our servers. All processing happens locally in your browser for maximum security and privacy.

Cookie Policy and Tracking

We utilize essential cookies and similar technologies to enhance site functionality and analyze usage patterns. You maintain control over cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that declining certain cookies may impact site functionality. For detailed information, please see our Cookie Policy.

Third-Party Service Providers

We partner with select third-party service providers for essential business operations. These partners are contractually bound to strict data protection standards and may only process your information for specified purposes under our direction.

Children's Privacy Protection

Our services are not designed for or directed at individuals under 13 years of age. We strictly prohibit the collection of personal information from children. If we discover any inadvertently collected children's data, we will promptly delete it.

Policy Updates

We regularly review and update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices and legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated through our website and/or email notifications. Continued use of our services following such updates constitutes acceptance of the modified policy.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access to your personal information
  • Correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion of your personal information
  • Restriction of processing
  • Data portability
  • Objection to processing

Contact Information

For privacy-related inquiries or concerns, please contact our Data Protection team at privacy@devtools.com. We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 business hours.
