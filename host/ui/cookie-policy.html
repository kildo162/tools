Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2025

What Are Cookies

At DevTools, we use cookies and similar technologies to provide you with a secure and personalized experience. Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They help us maintain the security of our development tools and ensure your preferences are remembered.

How We Use Cookies

We carefully select and implement cookies for the following essential purposes:

Essential Security Cookies

These cookies are crucial for our development tools. They help maintain the security of your session, prevent unauthorized access, and ensure your generated data remains private. Disabling these cookies will affect the core functionality of our service.

Performance & Analytics

We use minimal analytics to understand how developers use our tools and identify potential improvements. All data collection is anonymous and focused on enhancing our features and user experience.

Developer Preferences

These cookies remember your preferred algorithm choices, formatting settings, and UI customizations to streamline your workflow. They help maintain your development environment preferences between sessions.

Specific Cookies We Use

Cookie Name Purpose Duration
devtools-preferences Stores your tool preferences and settings 30 days

Cookie Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect cookie data. All cookies containing sensitive information are encrypted and accessible only through secure HTTPS connections. We regularly audit our cookie usage to ensure compliance with security best practices.

Managing Cookie Settings

While you can control cookies through your browser settings, please note that disabling essential cookies will impact the security and functionality of our development tools. We recommend keeping essential cookies enabled for the best secure experience.

Browser Cookie Settings

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Privacy, search, and services → Cookies

Third-Party Services

We minimize the use of third-party cookies. Currently, we only implement:

  • Essential security monitoring services to protect our development tools infrastructure
  • Anonymous usage analytics to improve our features and developer experience

Local Storage and Session Storage

In addition to cookies, our tools may use local storage and session storage to:

  • Save your work in progress (stored locally in your browser)
  • Cache tool configurations for better performance
  • Store temporary data that never leaves your browser

This data is stored locally and is never transmitted to our servers, ensuring maximum privacy and security.

Policy Updates

We regularly review and update our Cookie Policy to maintain the highest security standards. Any changes will be posted here with an updated revision date. Continuing to use our service after changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

Questions?

If you have any questions about our Cookie Policy or security practices, please contact our security team at security@devtools.com
